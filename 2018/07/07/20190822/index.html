I guess I will let it go when...

Date: July 7, 2018  23:17:55

Last night I had a dream, a dream that I wish it could be true.

I dreamed about a friend that I should never lose but actually lost, in my dream I was too shy or too guilty to talk to her so I just ran in front of her and she just called my name…and said

“I thought you won’t show up, otherwise my Lego gift for you would be useless”

and she gave the gift to me then said “and a chance that we can be friends once again”

And we just back to where we were, we are friends once again just like the old time but I woke up.

My consciousness was still unclear but I knew I was pretty sad just after I woke up from that dream.

I eventually have a chance to talk to her in April, she shows the indifference I have never seen before but I don’t blame her cause I know it was my fault, well most of my fault. I hurt her so badly, and now she refuses to talk to me or meet me and for that, I got nothing to complain I just deserve that.
Although I did everything I can to apologize and convince her back or even beg her, she just refused me once and once again, I know she’s afraid of I am gonna hurt her once again since the last time I did break her heart.

I won’t hurt her again, but it’s all too late to say.

after a month it’s her birthday so I decided to try the last time. I sent her the album of her favorite singer as a gift and wrote a short letter, I was hoping she can at least accept my gift. But a month later before I was about leaving for home I got the message that she also refused to sign for the gift, the postman asked me if I want the package back.

Finally, I decided to let it go.

Should I let it go?

Can I let it go?

I am not sure about that.

I guess I will let it go when I stopped dreaming about you.
